The Atelier of
Forgotten Tales

MYTHRAL was born from a quiet rebellion against mass production — a sanctuary for those who seek objects with soul, story, and slow intention. We are curators, nomads, and guardians of craft traditions that whisper across generations.

Our name draws from an ancient lexicon, meaning "eternal radiance" — a quality we believe lives inside every hand-shaped vessel, every handwoven textile, every talisman that finds its way to you.

✦ The Three Vows ✦

Hand-Honored

Every artifact is made by human hands — no assembly lines, only artisan-led workshops where skill is passed through blood and apprenticeship.

Earth-Conscious

We source natural, recycled, or upcycled materials. Our packaging is plastic-free, and we offset carbon for every shipment.

Myth-Kept

Each piece arrives with a story card — the legend behind its creation, the maker’s name, and a ritual to welcome it home.

⌇ The Potter’s Covenant

In the misty highlands of Himachal, we met Fatima Begum — a ceramicist who fires her clay in ancient pit kilns using wild clays gathered from seasonal riverbeds. Her hands remember what machines cannot replicate.

Clay speaks if you listen. I shape it, but the fire decides the final poem.

Fatima’s “Whispering Urn” and “Moonphase Vessel” are part of our permanent archive. For every piece sold, MYTHRAL funds a week of clean drinking water in her village. This is not charity — it is reciprocity.
