The mythos behind the objects

MYTHRAL was born from a reverence for forgotten crafts, slow rituals, and the beauty of the imperfect. We work directly with nomadic artisans, ceramicists from remote villages, and textile keepers who weave stories into every thread.

Our name echoes the old word “mythral” — a legendary alloy said to glow under moonlight. We believe that well-made objects carry that same luminance.
