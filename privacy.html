The Privacy Grimoire

How we honor, guard, and respect your celestial data

Effective: June 12, 2026 · Last revised: June 12, 2026

1. A Sacred Trust

At MYTHRAL ("we", "us", "the Emporium"), your privacy is not just a legal obligation — it is a sacred trust. This Privacy Grimoire explains what information we gather when you browse our artifacts, create an account, or make a purchase, and how we protect that information. By using our services, you entrust us with your data; we promise to treat it with the same reverence we give to handwoven heirlooms.

Our core promise: We never sell your personal information. Ever. Your data is used only to fulfill orders, improve your experience, and send you occasional mythic dispatches (only if you opt in).

2. What We Gather

Depending on how you interact with MYTHRAL, we may collect the following categories of information:

Identity Data

Name, billing/shipping address, email, phone number.

Transaction Data

Order history, payment details (processed via secure gateways).

Technical Data

IP address, browser type, device identifiers, cookies.

Communication Data

Newsletter preferences, support inquiries, feedback.

We never intentionally collect sensitive data (health, biometric, political beliefs) unless you explicitly volunteer it in a customer service message.

3. Cookies & Digital Sigils

Like most emporiums, we use cookies and similar tracking technologies to remember your cart, analyze site traffic, and personalize your journey. You can control cookie preferences through your browser settings, but disabling them may affect functionality (e.g., your basket might forget its treasures).

Types of cookies we use:

  • Essential cookies: Required for checkout, security, and account login.
  • Analytics cookies: Help us understand which collections captivate our visitors (via anonymized data).
  • Preference cookies: Remember your language and region.

Third-party services (like payment processors or shipping carriers) may place their own cookies — please review their policies separately.

4. How We Use Your Information

Every piece of data we collect serves a specific, transparent purpose:

  • Fulfillment: Process payments, pack and ship artifacts, provide order updates.
  • Customer care: Answer your questions, resolve issues, process returns.
  • Improvement: Analyze trends to curate better collections and enhance site performance.
  • Marketing (only with consent): Send you newsletter, early access to lunar drops, and artisan stories. You can unsubscribe anytime via a single click.
  • Legal compliance: Detect fraud, comply with tax laws, or respond to lawful requests.
Your choice matters: You can opt out of marketing emails at any time using the "unsubscribe" link in our emails or by contacting rites@mythral.com.

5. When We Share Data (Limited & Ethical)

We do not sell or rent your personal information. However, we may share your data with trusted partners strictly necessary to operate MYTHRAL:

  • Payment processors (Stripe, PayPal) — they handle your payment details securely and do not retain them longer than needed.
  • Shipping carriers (DHL, FedEx, local couriers) — to deliver your artifacts.
  • Analytics providers (such as privacy-respecting tools) — to understand site usage without identifying individuals.
  • Legal authorities — only if required by law or to protect our rights, property, or safety.

All partners are contractually bound to keep your data confidential and use it only for the specified services.

6. Data Security & Retention

We implement industry-standard safeguards: SSL encryption, restricted access to personal data, regular security audits. While no system is 100% invulnerable, we continuously update our protections.

Retention: We keep your personal data only as long as necessary — typically for 5 years after your last interaction (for tax and warranty purposes), after which it is anonymized or deleted. You may request deletion earlier (see Section 8).

7. Children's Privacy

MYTHRAL's artifacts are designed for adults and collectors over the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 16. If you believe a minor has provided us with personal data, please contact us so we can delete it promptly.

8. Your Rights (The Invocation of Control)

Depending on your location (especially if you reside in the EEA, UK, or California), you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access: Receive a copy of the data we hold about you.
  • Rectification: Correct inaccurate or incomplete information.
  • Erasure ("Right to be Forgotten"): Request deletion of your data, subject to legal obligations (e.g., tax records).
  • Restriction: Limit how we use your data.
  • Portability: Transfer your data to another service.
  • Objection: Opt out of direct marketing or certain processing.

To exercise any of these rights, email rites@mythral.com with the subject line "Privacy Invocation". We will respond within 30 days. We may ask for verification of identity before fulfilling your request.

Cross-border transfers: MYTHRAL is based in India. If you are visiting from outside India, your data will be transferred to and processed in India. We ensure appropriate safeguards (standard contractual clauses) for international data flows.

9. Third-Party Links & Embedded Content

Our product stories may include links to artisan profiles or external platforms (Instagram, etc.). Those sites have their own privacy policies, and we are not responsible for their practices. Always review their policies before sharing data.

10. Changes to This Grimoire

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally to reflect new practices or legal requirements. The "Effective date" at the top indicates the latest revision. If we make material changes, we will notify you via email (if you have an account) or through a notice on our homepage. Your continued use of MYTHRAL after changes constitutes acceptance.

For any privacy-related questions, concerns, or data requests, please reach out to our Data Guardian:
